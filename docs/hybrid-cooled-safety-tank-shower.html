Emergency Tank Shower

Hybrid Cooled Safety Tank Shower
ZEC1200 and ZEC1500

The hybrid chiller-based safety shower combines cutting-edge technology with energy efficiency. It ensures a consistent and comfortable water temperature, even in extreme conditions. This tank shower is designed with sustainability in mind, the chiller system minimizes energy consumption, reducing both operational costs and environmental impact.

  • Description
  • Specifications
  • Downloads

ECO series hybrid chiller models are designed to cool safety shower tanks ranging from 350 liters to 2000 liter to provide tepid water for emergency use. Unit can be used in explosive atmospheres (Zone 1, Zone 2) and is ATEX compliant.

To achieve a small and incredibly low power design, this device makes use of a novel hybrid technology that is covered by a patented and combines active cooling, passive cooling, and phase change materials (PCM). This chiller has fewer moving parts and more safety safeguards to guarantee high reliability. It can maintain water temperature at 25°C ± 3°C even in the hottest ambient. Extremely quiet operation, no footprint on the ground and slim design are added benefits of our system.

Environment Suitable for both Zone 1 and Zone 2
Capacity 350 L to 2000 L
Total Power Consumption < 400 Watts
Tank Water Temperature Setting +25°C to +36°C
Power Supply 220 (± 30V) / 1 or 3 Ph / 50 or 60 Hz
Run Current < 2A
Ambient Limits -5°C to +60°C
Compliance Meet ANSI Z358.1 requirement
Warranty Three years from the date of delivery
