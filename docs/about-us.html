About US

We provide green, sustainable and efficient solutions for industries.

Overview

Discover a new era of efficiency and sustainability with our revolutionary line of products, where excellence meets innovation. At SMITH APPLIED, we take pride in offering cutting-edge solutions that redefine industry standards.

Smith Applied is a community of creative and passionate engineers who thrive on innovation. Our mission is to deliver green, sustainable, and efficient solutions to various industries. Recognizing the industry’s commitment to sustainability, we envision providing cooling solutions that leave a minimal or zero CO2 footprint. At the heart of our approach lies the pathbreaking FastCool passive cooling technology, which achieves cooling without relying on electricity. We actively engage with our customers, seeking collaborative solutions to their sustainability challenges through our eco-friendly designs. Together, we forge a path toward a greener and more sustainable future.

As a USA-based start-up, we at Smith Applied understand the unique challenges posed by climate change. Our journey began with the simple yet profound idea of providing an eco-friendly solution to cool the safety tank showers without electricity, especially in challenging environments like the Middle East's hot climate. We draw inspiration from nature and apply ingenious engineering to deliver this greener alternatives. Our team actively seeks ways to reduce our carbon footprint and minimize environmental impact in the design and development of our solutions.
